## RISK MITIGATION & CHALLENGES

### POTENTIAL CHALLENGES

1. **Cash Flow**
   - Solution: Require 50% deposit upfront
   - Build 3-month emergency fund
   - Stagger equipment purchases

2. **Competition**
   - Solution: Niche specialization
   - Superior customer service
   - Unique Kenyan perspective
   - Fast turnaround times

3. **Equipment Failure**
   - Solution: Warranty purchases
   - Equipment maintenance fund
   - Backup partnerships

4. **Client Acquisition**
   - Solution: Aggressive early marketing
   - Portfolio building projects (discounted)
   - Networking & partnerships
   - Consistent social media

5. **Scaling Too Fast**
   - Solution: Measured growth approach
   - Hire only when revenue supports
   - Lease before buying expensive equipment
   - Test markets before full investment

6. **Skill Gaps**
   - Solution: Online courses (YouTube, Udemy)
   - Workshops & mentorship
   - Hire for weakness areas
   - Continuous learning budget
